Hello! My name is Madison Lyznick and I am a graduate from UC Davis, Class of 2020, with a double major in Cinema Digital Media and English - with an emphasis in creative writing. I thrive in a fast paced environment, adapt quickly to new situations, am passionate about new projects, and attribute acute attention to detail. I am proficient in Adobe Photoshop, HTML, CSS, Final Draft, Final Cut Pro, Microsoft Suite, and Davinci Resolve. My accomplishments include entering in writing challenges including NaNoWriMo (national novel writing month), writing 3 student films as well as a series of short stories entitled “Bar Soap”, “Tend the Fields”, “Leftovers”, etc.
